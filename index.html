Token Basics

Initially 333,333 $YOINK was issued on the XRPL. This was airdropped to those who set the Trustline, by the deadline and distributed based on $XRP holdings.

Clawback is set on $YOINK, as per the XRPL ammendment: https://xrpl.org/clawback.html
This means as the issuer, we are able to retrieve tokens from user wallets, YOINK!

The YOINK Game

As a meme token, we have designed a fun and engaging way to utilize the Clawback Amendment
Once an hour, a Yoinking commences following these rules:

  • Anyone holding less than 1 $YOINK & more than 0, will have their $YOINK clawed-back (YOINK!) This is permanently removed from supply.
  • 33 Wallets will have 1 $YOINK yoinked from their holdings (GET YOINKED!)
  • One lucky holder will receive 33% of Yoinked Holdings!
  • Team & Community wallets share 33% of Yoinked Holdings!
  • The remainder (34%) is permanently removed from the supply!
