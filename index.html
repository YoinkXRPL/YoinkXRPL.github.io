The YOINK Game

As a meme token, we have designed a fun and engaging way to utilize the Clawback Amendment
Once an hour, a Yoinking commences following these rules:

  • Anyone holding less than 1 $YOINK & more than 0, will have their $YOINK clawed-back (YOINK!) This is permanently removed from supply.
  • 33 Wallets will have 1 $YOINK yoinked from their holdings (GET YOINKED!)
  • One lucky holder will receive 33% of Yoinked Holdings!
  • Team & Community wallets share 33% of Yoinked Holdings!
  • The remainder (34%) is permanently removed from the supply!

YOINK & YEET

$YEET Token

YEET is the sister token to YOINK. It does what YOINK cannot, but those who YEET to hard, may be YOINKED!

  • Initially 300,000 $YEET was issued, 90% of YOINK.
  • YEET can only be obtained via the DEX, permanent offers will allow $YOINK holders to swap $YOINK for $YEET at .90 Exchange rate.
  • This means you could sell 10 $YOINK and you would receive minimum 9 $YEET. Permanently forever.

Through these permanent offers, both YEET & YOINK will slowly deflate in circulating supply. The excess will sit in the YOINK2YEET / YEET2YOINK wallet and will be used for burn events in future.

Why would I YEET?

Why wouldn't you?

  • YEET does not have clawback set - it's a safe place to hide some $YOINK
  • Because YEET does not have Clawback set, it will be posible to use YEET in the upcoming AMM Amendment
  • Holding Less YEET thank YOINK, but more YEET than 0 decreases your chances to be YOINKED!
  • The above reasons and more, will present interesting arbitrage opportunities in future

YEET/YOINK Ratio

It is important that one does not over YEET themselves, as such we have added some additional rules that affect the YOINK game.
If you hold less YEET thank YOINK, (but more than 0) you have less chance of being YOINKED than someone with only YOINK.
BUT, if you hold more YEET thank YOINK (and more than 0 YOINK), you have MORE chance of being YOINKED.

Each YOINK holder, is given a "weighted chance" to be picked.

  • Hold only YOINK, your Weighted chance is 1
  • Hold More YOINK thank YEET your weighted chance is LESS than 1
    • This is calculated as 1-((YEET/YOINK)*(YEET+YOINK)*0.000015)
  • Hold More YEET thank YOINK your weighted chance is MORE than 1
    • This is calculated as 1+((YEET/YOINK)*(YEET+YOINK)*0.000015)
